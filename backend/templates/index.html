About This Tool

How It Works

Our advanced prediction engine uses machine learning algorithms trained on historical AKTU counseling data to estimate your chances of admission in various colleges.

The model considers multiple factors:

  • Your rank compared to previous year cutoffs
  • Quota preferences (All India/Home State)
  • Category reservations
  • Gender-specific seats
  • Historical admission trends
  • Round-specific variations

Important Notes

Predictions are based on historical data and may vary in actual counseling.
Results are estimates—always check official counseling sources.

For the most accurate information, refer to the official AKTU counseling website.
